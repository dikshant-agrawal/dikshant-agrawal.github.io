About Dikshant Agrawal

Innovative Mechatronics Engineer

With a profound passion for innovation and a robust foundation in electronics, computer science, and mechanical engineering, I offer a diverse skill set honed through years of experience. My track record showcases a history of success in developing cutting-edge solutions that seamlessly integrate multiple disciplines. As an individual driven by curiosity and a thirst for knowledge, I continually strive to push the boundaries of what's possible in the field of Mechatronics Engineering.

How I Got Here

With a dynamic journey spanning multiple roles, I've honed my skills and expertise in diverse engineering domains. Beginning as a Hardware Design Engineer at EInfochips Pvt Ltd, I specialized in PCB design, power management circuits, and communication protocols under Arrow Electronics. Transitioning to Scientech Technologies Pvt. Ltd. as a Research and Development Engineer, I contributed to projects spanning educational training kits, smart energy metering, and IoT solutions, refining my ability to tackle complex challenges. Currently serving as a Junior Engineer at IITI DRISHTI CPS Foundation, I'm at the forefront of technological innovation, spearheading projects like the "Digital Health Kiosk," integrating computer vision and precision sensors to streamline patient registration. Additionally, I lead initiatives in digital twin development for critical care equipment, collaborating with esteemed institutions like AIIMS, New Delhi, and Cambridge University, UK. Passionate about mentoring researchers and advancing AI, ML, and embedded systems, I'm committed to delivering affordable technical solutions for end consumers.
