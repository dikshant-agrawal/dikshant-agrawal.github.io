About Me

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer with a strong academic foundation and hands-on experience spanning firmware, PCB design, and real-time systems. My work lives at the intersection of AI and embedded systems — focused on functional safety, energy-efficient architectures, and deterministic processing.

My path runs from designing buck converters for aerospace at Einfochips, to building life-saving healthcare instruments at IIT Indore's DRISHTI CPS Foundation, to writing Zigbee firmware for student racing cars at Hochschule Darmstadt.

I believe the best engineering is simple, testable, and humble about its assumptions — because in embedded, "there's no silver bullet."

+V · CURRENT FOCUS · GND

Currently → updating the CDD and communication stack of a Zigbee radio-controlled remote on student cars, plus researching edge-AI on Cortex-M for my master's.
