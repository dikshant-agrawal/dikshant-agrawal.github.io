01 / about

About Me

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer with a strong academic foundation and hands-on experience spanning firmware, PCB design, and real-time systems. I'm exploring the intersection of AI and embedded systems — focused on functional safety, energy-efficient architectures, and deterministic processing.

My path has taken me from designing buck converters for aerospace at Einfochips, to building healthcare devices that saved lives at IIT Indore's DRISHTI CPS Foundation, to developing firmware for student racing cars at Hochschule Darmstadt.

I believe the best engineering is simple, testable, and humble about its assumptions — because in embedded, "there's no silver bullet."
